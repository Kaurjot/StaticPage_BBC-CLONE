No Trident Debate at Labour Conference

Labour will not debate the case for scrapping Britain's Tridant nuclear weapons after the motion failed to get support from conference delegates.


The motion calls for "investment in people instead of these weapons of mass destruction". It comes after delegates at Labour's conference in Brighton voted last week to avoid a debate on the issue of Trident renewal.
